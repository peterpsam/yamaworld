The Mandala

Mandala is the last remaining stretch of land in Yama's world and home to what remains of the entire global population
A massive island country surrounded by oceans that stretch as far as the eye can see
This diverse and dense population of Mandala is divided into 4 levels vertically on an ascending mountainous terrain.

The Citizen Pass

One of the rarest pieces of tech you can possibly obtain in Mandala is a clean C-Pass with no criminal Records.
The Citizen Pass is a special body implant that grants access across multiple levels in Mandala. Including a secret underground meet-up spot known as “The Dhaba”.
Citizen Passes are a limited supply collectible available to early adopters of Yama. They will live on the Ethereum Blockchain as a unique non-fungible token.

The Dhaba

The Dhaba is only accessible to those that have obtained a multi-level Citizen Pass. The Dhaba is a place of connection
People from all walks of life sharing food, laughter, and culture in a secret underground location in one of the lower most levels of Mandala.
In here you'll find Dakus, the Jiva, and even some jaded enforcers downing Old Monkey on the rocks.
Fun Fact: The Dhaba is also the ONLY place in Mandala that is not surveilled by Yama or The Enforcers.
